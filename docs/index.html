Ragamuffin

"It's time to learn to survive on your own."

A 3D voxel survival-sandbox game set in modern-day Britain. Your parents have kicked you out. You start in a local park with nothing. Punch trees, gather resources, build shelter, and try to survive the night — all while dodging police, council builders, and gangs of youths in a grimly comedic British town.
Punch a tree to get wood.

VOXEL WORLD

Break and place blocks in a 3D urban environment. Greggs, charity shops, off-licences — it's all here.

SURVIVAL

Manage health, hunger, and energy. Build shelter before the police arrive at night.

CRAFTING

Craft tools and items from scavenged materials. Cardboard never looked so useful.

NPCS

Police, council builders, members of the public, dogs, and gangs of youths roam the streets.

Jewellers can be a good source of diamond.
